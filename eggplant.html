Eggplant Recipe


  • Ingredients
    • 1 medium eggplant
    • 2–3 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
    • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Steps
    • Wash and cut eggplant into cubes or slices.
    • Sprinkle with a little salt and let sit for 10 minutes (removes bitterness). Pat dry.
    • Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
    • Add garlic, sauté until fragrant.
    • Add eggplant pieces and cook, stirring often, until soft and golden (about 8–10 minutes).
    • Season with salt, pepper, and paprika/chili flakes.
    • Garnish with fresh herbs.

    ✨ Serve as a side dish, on toast, or mixed with pasta.
