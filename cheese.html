Indian Cheese Curry Recipe


  • Ingredients
    • 1 liter full cream milk
    • 2–3 tablespoons lemon juice or vinegar
    • 1 cup cold water
    • Muslin cloth or cheesecloth
  • Steps
    • Boil the milk in a heavy-bottomed pan, stirring occasionally.
    • When it comes to a boil, lower the flame and add lemon juice or vinegar gradually.
    • Stir gently until the milk curdles and separates into curds and whey.
    • Switch off the flame and add cold water to stop cooking.
    • Strain the mixture using a muslin cloth placed over a strainer.
    • Rinse the curds under running water to remove the sour taste.
    • Gather the cloth, squeeze extra water, and place a heavy weight on top to set.
    • After 1–2 hours, cut into cubes – fresh paneer is ready.

    ✨ Serve as a side dish, on toast, or mixed with pasta.
