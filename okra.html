Okra Recipe


  • Ingredients
    • 250 grams okra (bhindi), washed and chopped
    • 2 tablespoons oil
    • 1 medium onion, sliced
    • 1 medium tomato, chopped
    • 1–2 green chilies, slit
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
    • Salt to taste
    • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Steps
    • Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
    • Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
    • Mix in tomatoes and green chilies, cook until tomatoes turn soft.
    • Add turmeric, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt. Stir well.
    • Add chopped okra and cook on medium flame, stirring occasionally.
    • Cover and cook until okra becomes tender and well-coated with spices.
    • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.

    ✨ Serve as a side dish, on toast, or mixed with pasta.
